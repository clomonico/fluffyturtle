# Discovery Questions — Approval Process

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Source request:** "Our approval process is killing us. It takes too long, too many people are involved, and things fall through the cracks. We need a modern system to replace what we have. Can you build it?" — VP of Operations
**Request classification:** Frozen Request (specific solution stated before problem is understood)

---

## Opening / Rapport Building

1. **"Tell me the story of the last approval that went sideways."** — Gets a concrete example instead of abstract frustration.
2. **"How long has this been a problem, and what changed to make it urgent now?"** — Separates chronic pain from a recent trigger event.

## Problem Discovery

3. **"When you say 'too long,' what does the timeline actually look like? Days? Weeks?"** — Puts numbers on the pain.
4. **"Where in the process do things stall most often?"** — Identifies the bottleneck vs. the whole chain.
5. **"What falls through the cracks — approvals that never get completed, or decisions that get made without the right people?"** — Two very different failure modes.
6. **"What happens when something does fall through? What's the business impact?"** — Quantifies the cost of the current state.
7. **"Is the problem that approvals take too long, or that people don't trust the outcome when they're done?"** — Tests whether speed is really the issue or if it's confidence.

## Stakeholder Ecosystem

8. **"Who are the people involved in a typical approval? Who added them, and why?"** — Surfaces whether the complexity is intentional governance or accumulated cruft.
9. **"Who gets blamed when an approval goes wrong?"** — Reveals accountability dynamics.
10. **"Is there anyone who would resist changing this process? What would concern them?"** — Finds the hidden stakeholders.

## Current State Deep Dive

11. **"Walk me through an approval from start to finish — what tools do you use, who touches it, where does it live?"** — Maps the actual workflow, not the documented one.
12. **"Are there approvals that work fine today? What makes those different?"** — Identifies what's already working so we don't break it.
13. **"Have you tried to fix this before? What happened?"** — Uncovers failed attempts and organizational scar tissue.

## Constraint Discovery

14. **"Are there compliance or regulatory requirements that dictate who must approve what?"** — Separates mandatory steps from optional ones.
15. **"What systems does this need to integrate with — ERP, procurement, HR?"** — Scopes the technical landscape.
16. **"If we could fix one thing about the process tomorrow, what would make the biggest difference?"** — Forces prioritization.

## Success Criteria

17. **"If we solve this, what does 'good' look like six months from now? What metrics would tell you it's working?"** — Defines the finish line before we start running.
18. **"Who else needs to believe this worked for it to actually stick?"** — Identifies whose buy-in we need beyond the VP.
